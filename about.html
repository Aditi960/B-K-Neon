The beginning

Where it all started.

It started in 2012, when Balkrishna Kadam — the "B.K." behind the name — hand-bent his first neon sign for a neighbourhood pharmacy in Shivajinagar. The sign stayed on for eight years without a repair. That durability became the foundation of everything we do.

What began as a one-man workshop grew into a full-service signage studio, adding LED technology without ever abandoning the hand-craft tradition that gives our signs their character. Every tube bend, every wire joint, every backboard cut is done in-house in our Pune workshop.

Today, B.K. Neon signs glow in café windows, wedding venues, brand offices, and living rooms from Mumbai to Bangalore to Delhi. But we're still the same studio — just bigger. Every sign we make is still custom. Still hand-made. Still Pune.

Our journey

12 YEARS
OF GLOW

2012
The first sign
Balkrishna Kadam hand-bends B.K. Neon's first sign for a pharmacy in Shivajinagar, Pune. One sign, one workshop, one dream.
2015
Café culture takes off
Koregaon Park's café boom brings B.K. Neon into the city's most photographed interiors. Neon becomes the signature of Pune's new food scene.
2017
Expanding to LED
We add LED channel lettering and backlit panels to our range — bringing energy efficiency and new design possibilities to commercial clients.
2019
Wedding neon explodes
B.K. Neon becomes a staple at high-end weddings across Maharashtra and Mumbai. Our script signs are now in hundreds of wedding photographs.
2021
Pan-India delivery
We begin safely shipping signs to clients across India — from Delhi offices to Bangalore studios to Goa beach bars. The Pune workshop goes national.
2024
500 signs and counting
We cross 500 custom signs delivered and launch our new online presence. Still hand-made. Still in Pune. Still lighting up India, one sign at a time.
